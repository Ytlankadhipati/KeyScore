How to Fill the Feedback Form

Your feedback is very important to us. Follow these simple steps to submit your feedback:

  1. Navigate to the Feedback Section of the website.
  2. Enter your Name (optional), Email (optional), and provide a rating based on your experience.
  3. In the Comments section, write your feedback or suggestions.
  4. Once you’ve filled in all the fields, click the Submit Feedback button.

If you need further assistance, feel free to reach out to us through the contact details below.

How to Upload Data

Uploading data is simple. Just follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Upload Section of the platform.
  2. Click the Choose File button to select the file you want to upload.
  3. Once the file is selected, click the Upload button to begin uploading.
  4. Your data will be processed and displayed once the upload is complete.

If you need help with uploading or encounter any issues, please contact support.
